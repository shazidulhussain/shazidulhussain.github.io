Research focus

Reading molecular events through color

PHASE

PDA phase behaviour & chromatic transitions

Thin films engineered so a specific binding event triggers a visible, quantifiable colour shift.

SENSE

Colorimetric sensor design

Sensors for bacteria, harmful gases, pesticides, food spoilage, and disease biomarkers, read by eye or by camera.

FRET

Ratiometric FRET sensing

Dual-signal readouts that correct for concentration and lighting artefacts in the field.

ELEC

Electrochemical sensing

Pairing optical response with electrochemical readout for confirmatory, quantitative detection.

SWITCH

Resistive switching & memristors

Memristor-based sensing elements that store, as well as report, a sensing event.

ASSEMBLY

LB films & nanoparticle hybrids

Langmuir–Blodgett films, PDA–nanoparticle hybrids, and supramolecular assemblies as the physical substrate for sensing.
